There are many different headsets out there!

This will cover the main players in the VR world.


Oculus

Oculus was one of the first companies to commercialize VR headsets by lowering the cost to own a headset.

HTC Vive

First released to consumers on April 2016, the HTC Vive was developed by HTC and Valve and is up there with Oculus as one of the best headsets out in the market.

PSVR

Developed by Sony Playstation the PSVR is Sony's way of putting thier hat in the "VR Ring" and go toe to toe with Oculus and the HTC Vive.
